About NB Aerial

Welcome to NB Aerial, I am Nate Blackman, founder of NB Aerial, a drone company specializing in aerial cinematography. My journey of drone flying began in 2017, fueled by a passion for racing. Coming from a background rooted in racing, I am ranked among the top 200 best pilots worldwide. My racing background not only signifies my commitment to excellence but also translates seamlessly into precision flying required for high-end aerial cinematography. At NB Aerial, we specialize in delivering high quality aerial production services. My skills, developed over years of flying and racing, bring a unique and precise touch to every project. From capturing breathtaking landscapes to your specific aerial needs, NB Aerial ensures a perfect blend of technical proficiency and creative innovation.
