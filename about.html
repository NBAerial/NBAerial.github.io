About NB Aerial

Founded by Nate Blackman, NB Aerial stands at the forefront of aerial production, transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary. Drawing from a unique background as an FPV drone racing pilot for the past three years, Nate brings an unparalleled level of precision to aerial cinematography. With a distinguished ranking among the top 150 FPV drone racing pilots globally, his expertise extends beyond the race track into the realm of capturing breathtaking visuals from the skies. Nate's racing prowess seamlessly translates into the art of precision flying, ensuring that every shot taken by NB Aerial is a testament to skill, innovation, and passion for pushing the boundaries of aerial cinematography.
